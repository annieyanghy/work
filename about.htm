About

I am Hsin-yu! I am a UX designer who loves to humanize inhumane problems. I had a background in humanity and literatures languages, which shaped my design value to focus on humans and creating emotions.

My background in foreign languages and literatures foster me become an empathetic UX designer because I care about contexts and stories of users. My experience of co-starting a sustainable vegan restaurant sparked my passion in user experience design to solve problems through design solutions and creating experiences.
